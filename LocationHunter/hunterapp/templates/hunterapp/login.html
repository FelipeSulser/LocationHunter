LocationHunter optimizes trips for photographers

Plan your next trip to a major city or to a wildlife park and see which hashtags are trending there. Find new travel buddies for your next trip. Find new followers that will help you increase instagram traffic.

How will LocationHunter help me improve my Instagram game?

Using data collected from your previous publications and the locations you want to visit, we provide a ranking of the hashtags that are the most relevant in that location from your perspective.

Recommending travel buddies is another feature of LocationHunter. This will help you grow your network of known photographers. Become known worldwide as the best photographer.

Know who you follow. LocationHunter provides a recommender system that will automatically pick followers that match with your type of photography style and are relevant. This will maximize your relevance as well increasing your chance of networking and reposting your images.
